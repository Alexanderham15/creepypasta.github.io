What is creepypasta?

Creepypasta is a series of internet horror stories. Their full of childhood fear, they are everything you would see in your nightmares as a kid



The origins of creepypasta

Creepypasta stories began on the site 4Chan as stories called 'copypasta' most of their stories started out as chainmail and over the years they developed.
Over the year these 'copypastas' developed into what we know today as creepypastas.
